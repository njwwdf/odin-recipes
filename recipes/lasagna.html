Italian Mama's Lasagna

This is the greatest lasagna recipe in the world. It was a secret passed down for generations, only now being shared with the world.

Ingredient List

  • pasta sheets
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • green leafy for garnish
  • big ol glass (or bottle) of wine

Recipe Steps

  1. Set pasta sheet in pan.
  2. Put cheese on sheet.
  3. Pour tomato sauce on top.
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 until you are out of pasta sheets.
  5. Put extra cheese on top and set in oven at 425F for 1 hour.
  6. Try to finish wine bottle before lasagna is done baking.
  7. Realize the lasagna tastes better when drunk.
