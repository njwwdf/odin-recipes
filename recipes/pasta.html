Just OK Pasta

This is the greatest pasta recipe in the world. It was a secret passed down for generations, only now being shared with the world.

Ingredient List

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 quart tap water
  • big ol glass (or bottle) of wine

Recipe Steps

  1. Bring water to boil in a pot.
  2. Pour noodles in water.
  3. Wait 7-10mins for noodles to become limp.
  4. Drain water with strainer.
  5. Pour tomato sauce over noodles.
  6. Put cheese on top.
  7. Drink 2 glasses of wine with dinner because you are classy.
  8. Lose all respect for self. Finish that bottle because Mama didn't raise a quitter.
