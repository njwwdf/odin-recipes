Big ol Pizza

This is the greatest pizza recipe in the world. It was a secret passed down for generations, only now being shared with the world.

Ingredient List

  • pizza dough
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • veggies for garnish
  • big ol glass (or bottle) of wine

Recipe Steps

  1. Set pizza dough on pan.
  2. Pour tomato sauce on dough.
  3. Put cheese on top.
  4. Put veggies on top of cheese.
  5. Put extra cheese on top and set in oven at 425F for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Try to finish wine bottle before pizza is done baking.
  7. Realize the pizza tastes so much better when drunk.
